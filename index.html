Rehab Has Never Been Better

Located in the heart of Downtown San Diego, what was once a 75,000sqft warehouse, is now home to the last health club membership you will ever need to purchase. Welcome to Rehab.

Main Gym FLoor

What would Rehab be if it did not feature a state of the art of gym equipment. Each piece of equipment is fully cross functional and allows our members to be able to engage in multiple excerises without having to move stations. It trully is a fitness enthusiast dream.

Dumbbell Row

Definitely a crowd pleaser, lined up against the south wall of the main gym floor are two seperate dumbbell racks. Starting from 10lbs and moving in increments of 5lbs, this rack is capped off with a pair 120lbs dumbbells. Do not worry we have 3 pairs of each weight.

Cardio Row

Lined up against the north wall of the main gym floor, even if you are not a big fan of cardio, the first row cardio equipment have a stunning view of Downtown San Diego. WHile the back row might be a obscured of this view, the 40in TV situated above them will provide some entertaining viewing.

Circuit Training

Don't have time? No need to worry, between the cardio row and dumbell row sits our fullbody circuit training area. Whether you decide to go for max reps or max weight, you will get a good workout in.

Player's Paradise

While your average gym might have a basketball court as well, we made sure to include bleachers around the court to ensure that even if you have to wait to a play a game, you will be comfortable. Having this seating also enables all our members more space, especially when we are funning leagues and tournaments.

Train and Relax

Accessed through the lockeroom, our pool area houses not only the pool but our rotating spa as well as a lounge area to simply relax. The pool is junior olympic size in terms of length; however, it does feature 3 lanes and a seperate wading area for those looking to just enjoy the water.

The Best Pick Me Up and Cool Down

Located after the Checkin Desk, Our Cafe provided meals and drinks that are good whether they are pre or post workout. You can alos hangout after and enjoy our complimentary WIFI to get some work done, or simply unwind.
